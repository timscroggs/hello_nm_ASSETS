web & Interactive Design

If you decide to follow the web + interactive track, prepare to have your mind blown! We blend big ideas with web design, interactivity and a splash of performance art. You will learn how to create, code, and publish content for any device while mastering HTML5, Javascript, CSS3.


If you are as passionate as we are about creating interactive experiences by fusing the digital world of technology and web design with the physical world art performance, come join us!

video Art & Design

The video art + design track here at UNCA you learn the industry from the ground up. This program is all about hands-on-experience. You’ll learn the preproduction process: creating story, scripts, to the production process: shooting, lighting, recording audio, finally the post-production process: cutting, rearranging, and enhancing footage, recording and editing additional dialogue, sound effects, music, as well as adding visual effects and titles. With all of that and more, there can't be a reason not to sign up!

Software

final cut pro + after effects + premiere pro + max msp jitter
